About Me

Hello! My name is Sakdipat “Jaf” Kongkaeo, a graduate student in Atmospheric & Oceanic Sciences at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. My academic interests center on climate variability, large-scale ocean–atmosphere interactions, and ENSO predictability using advanced linear inverse modeling methods.

Outside of research, I enjoy photography, baking, and learning guitar and bass.
